About HealthRisk

HealthRisk is an intelligent system designed to provide early risk assessments for common non-communicable diseases. By leveraging advanced machine learning models (TensorFlow), we aim to help individuals be more aware of their health status and encourage preventive measures. Our goal is to contribute to a healthier community by making health insights accessible and understandable.
